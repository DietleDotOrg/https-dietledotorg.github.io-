The Descendants of Johann Adam Dietel of 19th Century Somerset County, Pennsylvania

Introduction
The Descendants of Johann Adam Dietel, written by Lannie L. Dietle and Dana Moore in 1996, is available to download in its original format. This labor of love has served as a resource for relatives near and far, and is now being converted into a digital, interactive format by Lannie's daughter Emily Dietle. Additional information and supplementary materials can be found at Lannie's website. Corrections and Additions can be viewed in Addendum A.
