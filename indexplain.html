The Descendants of Johann Adam Dietel of 19th Century Somerset County, Pennsylvania

Introduction
The Descendants of Johann Adam Dietel of 19th Century Somerset County, Pennsylvania—written by Lannie L. Dietle and Dana Moore in 1996—is available to download in its original format. This labor of love has served as a resource for relatives near and far, and is now being converted into a digital, interactive format by Lannie's daughter Emily Dietle. Corrections and Additions can be viewed in Addendum A.

View the digital version of The Descendants of Johann Adam Dietel. Please note that as of November 6, 2025, the digitization is still in process, and available content is limited. In the future, the book will be available on the main site in a living digital format, with updates and corrections included throughout.

Additional information and supplementary materials can be found at Lannie Dietle's website.
